How it works

From your closet to a styled look in minutes

Four simple steps turn the clothes you already own into outfits you can see on yourself before you wear them.

1

Snap your clothes

Photograph each piece front and back. Drape removes the background and auto-tags its colour, pattern and category.

2

Create your digital you

Four quick selfies become a photoreal body model — your real proportions, from every angle.

3

Ask your stylist

Pick an occasion and Sandra composes a complete look from your wardrobe, matched to the weather and your colours.

4

Try it on

See the outfit rendered on you, front and back. Save it to your lookbook, wear it, and shop the pieces that complete it.

FAQ

Questions, answered

What is Drape?

Drape is an iOS app that combines a digital wardrobe, an AI personal stylist and a photoreal virtual fitting room. You build a wardrobe from clothes you own, see outfits rendered on a model of your own body, and get styling suggestions for any occasion.

Is the try-on really on my body?

Yes. During setup you take four quick selfies, which Drape turns into a photoreal body model with your real proportions. Outfits are rendered on that model — front and back — so what you see reflects how clothes sit on you, not on a generic mannequin.

Are my photos and data private?

Your photos are encrypted and used only to power your try-on. You can delete your body model and photos at any time from within the app. We never sell your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Where do the clothes come from?

Primarily your own wardrobe — you add the pieces you own. When a look needs something you don't have, Drape can suggest pieces from leading fashion retailers that you can try on virtually and then buy.

What does it cost?

Drape is free to start, with up to three items per category. Pro is US$9.99/month for an unlimited wardrobe and priority styling. You can cancel any time.

What do I need to use it?

An iPhone. Drape is currently in private beta on iOS via TestFlight, with a wider App Store launch in 2026.
