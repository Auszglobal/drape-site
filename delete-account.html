Delete your Nuvel account and data

This page explains how to request deletion of your Nuvel account and the personal data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open the Nuvel app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open account settings and choose Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and associated data are removed.

Option 2 — Request by email

Email info@nuvel.com.au from the address linked to your account and ask us to delete your account. We action verified requests promptly.

What is deleted

  • Your account and profile (name, email, sign-in details).
  • Photos you provided — selfies, body-scan images and wardrobe item photos.
  • Your generated try-on images and styling history.
  • App activity and preferences tied to your account.

What may be kept, and for how long

After deletion, residual copies may persist in encrypted backups for a short period and are then purged. We may retain a minimal record where required to meet legal, tax or fraud-prevention obligations, after which it is deleted.
